English

This form is for members of the Colorado community to offer skills, resources, supplies, space and time to community members who are affected by COVID-19 (coronavirus) and those most vulnerable among us.

We need to show up for each other. Especially for those who are at great risk (the elderly, the immunocompromised, those with chronic illnesses, the poor, unhoused folks, undocumented immigrants, those with little to no access to health care).

If you are looking to provide help, please fill out this form so we can connect you with those in need.

After being paired with someone, do not ask or give information about immigration status. If you are asked about your immigration status, please report it to Abolish ICE Denver.
